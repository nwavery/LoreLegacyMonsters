# Privacy Policy (Draft)

Last updated: 2026-05-04

This Privacy Policy explains how NA Dev handles data in Lore, Legacy, and Monsters.

## 1. Data processed locally

The Game stores gameplay data locally on your machine, including:

- save files (quest progress, party state, story flags),
- settings (graphics, controls, local AI preferences),
- optional local logs for diagnostics.

## 2. Local AI processing

The Game can run a bundled local language model runtime for NPC dialogue.

- NPC dialogue inference runs on your device.
- Prompts used for local AI dialogue are not sent to NA Dev servers by default.
- You can disable local AI in settings.

## 3. Data we receive

NA Dev does not receive personal gameplay data by default through the standalone game client.

If optional crash reporting is enabled in a future update, this policy will be updated with exact fields and retention.

## 4. Third-party platforms

If you use Steam:

- Steam may collect platform account, entitlement, commerce, and analytics data under Valve's policies.
- Steam Cloud usage (if enabled) is governed by Valve platform terms.

Please review Steam's privacy policy for platform-level processing.

## 5. Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect any data under our control. No transmission or storage method is perfectly secure.

## 6. Children's privacy

The Game is not directed to children under the minimum age required by local law and storefront policy.

## 7. Your rights

Depending on jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict personal data. Contact us at support@example.com.
