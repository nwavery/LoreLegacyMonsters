# Steam Store Description Draft

## Short description

Explore a cozy pixel monster RPG where your choices reshape the story and NPCs can answer follow-up questions using a local AI model running on your own machine.

## Full description

Lore, Legacy, and Monsters is a story-rich monster-catching RPG with branching outcomes, tactical turn-based battles, and a hand-crafted world that expands across multiple chapters.

- Build your team by capturing and training unique monsters.
- Choose how major conflicts resolve and unlock different endings.
- Explore a growing map with quests, rivals, mentors, and hidden routes.
- Battle through elemental matchups, guard timing, and capture risk decisions.
- Talk to NPCs with authored story lines plus optional local AI follow-up dialogue.

### Local AI feature disclosure

NPC follow-up dialogue can be generated at runtime by a bundled local model. Inference runs locally on your device; player prompt text is not sent to our servers for generation. Story-critical progression remains deterministic in scripted systems, and the AI feature can be disabled in settings.

### Minimum hardware guidance (draft)

- OS: Windows 10/11 64-bit
- CPU: 4-core desktop/mobile CPU
- RAM: 16 GB recommended for smooth local AI usage
- GPU: integrated or dedicated (VRAM helps with responsiveness)
- Storage: SSD recommended, with additional space for local model files
